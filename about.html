About Geordie Geneaology

Geordie Geneaology is a geneaological project based out of Newcastle upon Tyne and ran by Ian Royal.
A long time family tree hobbyist, Ian loves to delve into the history of his own family tree, and wants to help others do the same.
Bolstered with the skills of many years tracing his own family tree, let Ian aid you in uncovering your family's past.
